Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Culinary Club's Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our reservation concierge service.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Reservation preferences and details
  • Communication opt-in preferences
  • Device information and usage data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our reservation concierge service
  • Communicate with you about your reservations
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Text Messaging and Mobile Information

Important: We do not share your mobile information, phone number, or text messaging data with any third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Your text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties.

We may share your information with subcontractors solely for the purpose of providing customer service related to your reservations. These subcontractors are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

Third-Party Lead Generation

Culinary Club does not participate in third-party lead generation activities. We do not collect or generate customer information to be shared with other businesses. Your personal information, including contact details and opt-in preferences, will not be shared outside of Culinary Club with partners, affiliates, third parties, or other businesses that you have not directly opted in to receive SMS from or have your information shared with.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Opt out of text message communications

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
